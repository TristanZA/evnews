CNBM and Geely SA Drive Clean Energy Forward

Tristan Wiggill March 29, 2026 news

China National Building Materials (CNBM) South Africa and Geely Auto South Africa have signed a Strategic Cooperation Framework Agreement to work together on clean energy and electric mobility projects across South Africa and the wider Southern African region. The agreement was signed at the Future Energy Storage Exhibition in Johannesburg on 25 March 2026.

What the partnership is about

CNBM South Africa specialises in solar power and battery energy storage systems. Geely Auto South Africa focuses on electric vehicles (EVs) and vehicle-to-grid (V2G) technology, which allows electric cars to feed power back into the electricity grid.

Together, the two companies will develop an integrated network of clean energy solutions, covering solar generation, battery storage, EV charging infrastructure and green supply chains. The goal is to make clean energy more accessible and practical for South African consumers and businesses.

Why this matters for South Africa

South Africa is working to reduce its reliance on fossil fuels and build a more reliable, sustainable energy system. This partnership directly supports those goals by bringing together two complementary sets of expertise under one shared vision.

The collaboration also supports local industrial development. Both companies are committed to building localised supply chains, which means more goods and services will be sourced and produced within South Africa. This creates opportunities for local businesses and helps develop skills within the country.

What they will build together

Under the agreement, CNBM South Africa and Geely South Africa will work together across several key areas.They will coordinate the rollout of new energy vehicles alongside the charging infrastructure needed to support them.

They will also implement solar-storage-charging solutions, where solar panels, battery storage and EV chargers work together as a single system. In addition, both companies will focus on developing local supply chains to support long-term growth and sustainability.

The initial focus will be on South Africa, with plans to expand across Southern Africa as the partnership develops.

Voices from the partnership

"This agreement marks an important step in our mission to bring practical, integrated clean energy solutions to South Africa," said a spokesperson for CNBM South Africa. "By working with Geely South Africa, we can offer a much more complete solution to our customers and partners."

A spokesperson for Geely Auto South Africa added, "Electric vehicles are only part of the answer. When you combine them with solar power and smart storage, you create something far more powerful. That is exactly what this partnership makes possible."

A shared commitment to sustainability

Both companies have expressed a strong commitment to environmental and social responsibility. The partnership is designed not only to deliver commercial results but also to contribute meaningfully to South Africa's sustainable development.

CNBM South Africa has been active in the South African market for a number of years, building expertise in green energy technologies and developing strong relationships with local partners. Geely Auto South Africa brings global experience in electric mobility and a growing presence in the South African automotive market.

CNBM South Africa and Geely South Africa will begin practical collaboration in the coming months, with joint projects expected to be announced as the partnership progresses. Both companies see South Africa as a key market and are committed to playing a long-term role in the country's clean energy future.
