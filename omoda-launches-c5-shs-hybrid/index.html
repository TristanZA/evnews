OMODA launches C5 SHS hybrid

Tristan Wiggill April 8, 2026 New Models

OMODA South Africa has added the C5 SHS to its local vehicle range. The new model is a hybrid variant of the C5 and is the third electrified OMODA available in South Africa. It is also the first conventional hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) in the OMODA and JAECOO lineup, as the brand's other electrified models are plug-in hybrids.

The C5 SHS is powered by a turbocharged 1.5-litre four-cylinder petrol engine paired with an electric motor. Combined output stands at 165 kW and 295 N.m of torque. Drive goes to the front wheels through a Dedicated Hybrid Transmission (DHT). The vehicle is reported to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in 7.9 seconds and has a claimed top speed of 175 km/h.

Fuel consumption is rated at 4.9 L/100 km on the WLTP cycle, with CO2 emissions of 111 g/km. A 1.83 kWh lithium iron phosphate battery works alongside a 51-litre fuel tank to give the C5 SHS a claimed range of more than 1,000 km.

Suspension consists of a MacPherson strut setup at the front and a multilink arrangement at the rear. The C5 SHS rides on 18-inch alloy wheels with 215/55 tyres, and disc brakes are fitted at all four corners, with ventilated discs at the front.

Standard equipment includes a power-operated boot lid, automatic windscreen wipers, 60/40 split-folding rear seats, a 50W wireless phone charger, keyless entry with push-button start, roof rails, and power-adjustable side mirrors with auto-fold.

Boot capacity is 372 litres, expanding to 1,072 litres with the rear seats folded. Automatic LED headlights, LED daytime running lights, a sunroof, and red-painted brake calipers are also fitted as standard.

Inside, the C5 SHS has a symmetrical dashboard layout and a bridge-style centre console. Dual 12.3-inch screens are fitted, with the touchscreen supporting Bluetooth, wireless Android Auto, and wireless Apple CarPlay. An eight-speaker Sony sound system, intelligent voice command, multi-colour ambient lighting, and acoustic glass for the front occupants are all included.

Front seats are sports-style units trimmed in black leather, with six-way power adjustment, heating, and ventilation for both the driver and front passenger. Dual-zone climate control and rear air vents are also standard.

The C5 SHS carries a five-star Euro NCAP rating, first achieved by the C5 in 2022. Safety equipment includes seven airbags, ISOFIX child seat anchorages, electronic stability programme, and electronic brake-force distribution. Driver assistance systems include front and rear parking sensors, a 360-degree camera, blind-spot detection, rear cross-traffic alert, and lane change assist.

The C5 SHS is available in four solid colours — Bloodstone Red, Carbon Crystal Black, Khaki White, and Phantom Grey — as well as two dual-tone options pairing Carbon Crystal Black with either Aerial Silver or Khaki White.

The C5 SHS is priced at R479,900, with an introductory price of R469,900 for the first month. The purchase price includes a five-year/75,000 km service plan, a five-year/150,000 km factory warranty, and a 10-year/one-million km engine warranty linked to the first owner. The model is available at OMODA and JAECOO dealerships from 8 April 2026.
